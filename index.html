OpenURLs.net

A simple tool to open multiple URLs with a customizable delay

How to Use

  1. Enter your URLs (one per line)
  2. Adjust the delay between opens (0.1 to 5 seconds)
  3. Choose to open in new tabs or windows
  4. Click 'Open URLs' to start

Browser Authorization: Allow OpenURLs.net to open multiple tabs in your browser settings

Frequently Asked Questions

Where Can You Use OpenURLs?

OpenURLs is a one-click, no-install tool compatible with all modern web browsers. Works seamlessly on Windows, Linux, macOS, iOS, and Android devices, including computers, smartphones, and tablets.

Is There a Limit to URL Openings?

While there's no strict limit, we recommend opening 20-25 URLs at a time for optimal browser performance. Opening too many URLs simultaneously may cause temporary browser slowdown.

Is OpenURLs Safe to Use?

Absolutely! OpenURLs is a client-side tool that runs entirely in your browser. We don't store, collect, or transmit any of your personal data. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

Mobile Device Compatibility

OpenURLs is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. The interface adapts to your screen size, ensuring a smooth experience across smartphones and tablets.
